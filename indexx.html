About Me

The Eye
Behind the Lens

I'm Adnan Gohar, a freelance photographer based in Pakistan with a deep passion for visual storytelling. My work explores the intersection of light, shadow, and human emotion — finding beauty in both grand landscapes and quiet, unguarded moments.

With over a decade of experience, I've worked with editorial clients, brands, and private commissions across South Asia and beyond. Whether it's a portrait that reveals a soul, a cityscape at golden hour, or the raw energy of street life — I believe every photograph holds a secret worth telling.
